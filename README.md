# Quantum Computing for Students

This repository is created to help **students and beginners** learn the fundamentals of **Quantum Computing** in a simple and practical way.

The main goal of this repository is to make quantum computing **accessible to students from small cities and developing regions**, where access to advanced labs and resources is limited. The material here is designed to start from basic concepts and gradually move toward practical implementation.

---

## What You Will Learn
- Introduction to quantum computing
- Linear algebra basics for quantum systems
- Qubits, superposition, and measurement
- Quantum gates and quantum circuits
- Basic quantum algorithms
- Hands-on practice using **Qiskit**
- Simulators and introduction to quantum hardware

---

## Tools and Technologies
- Python
- Qiskit
- IBM Quantum Platform
- Jupyter Notebooks

---

## Who This Repository Is For
- Undergraduate and graduate students
- Beginners interested in quantum computing
- Educators and instructors
- Researchers starting practical quantum work

No prior experience in quantum computing is required.

---

## Teaching and Community Focus
This repository is also used for **teaching workshops and lab sessions**, especially for students who do not have the opportunity to study in large cities or specialized research centers. The aim is to **build confidence, skills, and interest** in quantum technologies.
